Executive Boardroom

The Plaza Executive Boardroom is located on the 11th floor and provides the perfect space for high-level meetings, offering you and your delegates both
privacy and security. The boardroom accommodates up to 16 executives and features a large conference table with padded executive chairs, the latest AV equipment,
non-fixed furniture for full flexibility, and a customized catering menu perfect for conference lunches.
A dedicated Meeting & Events Specialist will assist with all planning details.
